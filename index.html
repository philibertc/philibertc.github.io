About me

I am an electronics enthusiast, without much knowledge but I am curious and I like to learn. Electronics and programming are complementary and I am also interested in this field. I like everything that revolves around the Arduino and especially home automation.

My most popular projects

  • The MicroNova Controller: this is my biggest project and clearly the most successful one, over 400 people have visited the page for this one! This is a WiFi MQTT pellet stove controller, compatible with over 30 different brands. I have created a circuit board for this project which is very much to the satisfaction of those who have purchased it.
  • The Arduino Ambilight: I am far from being the only one to have done this kind of project, but in my versions I always take care to explain the steps to follow in order to do it easily at home. It’s about creating a system similar to Ambilight but for only $30!
